## Vision Statement

> Build the most trusted, real-time ERP for Uzbekistan's wholesale and retail businesses — where every sale, every payment, and every product is visible instantly across every device, in both UZS and USD, with the accuracy that real enterprises demand.

---

## Product Principles

1. **Real-time first** — If it's not instant, it's not done
2. **Currency honest** — UZS and USD treated as equals, history never rewritten
3. **FIFO faithful** — Profit is truth, calculated not guessed
4. **Company sacred** — Data isolation is non-negotiable
5. **Admin empowered** — Full remote control over users, devices, modules
6. **Modular forever** — Today's feature is tomorrow's plugin
